Pocket
Chop

Sample slicer and step sequencer for iPad. Built entirely in Swift Playgrounds.

Platform iPad · iPadOS 18.1+
Year 2026
Award Swift Student Challenge — WWDC 2026
Stack SwiftUI · AVFoundation · UIKit
Try on TestFlight ↗

What it does

Import any audio file, slice it into up to 16 named regions, assign each to a pad, and build patterns in a 64-step sequencer. Includes a piano roll, per-pad FX and ADSR envelopes, and full-mix recording — all offline, no internet required. Design language inspired by teenage engineering hardware.

Why I built it

I wanted a real production tool that ran entirely on iPad — no laptop, no subscription. PocketChop sits at the centre of the three things I do: sound, engineering precision, and iOS craftsmanship.
